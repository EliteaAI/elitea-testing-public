# QA Handbook

## Overview

The QA Handbook provides an overview of the QA team, our mission, and how we work.

## Team Information

### Mission
Ensure the Elitea platform delivers high quality, reliable, and user-friendly AI experiences.

### Responsibilities
- Test planning and execution
- Bug reporting and triage
- Test automation
- Quality metrics and reporting
- Process improvement

## Team Structure

### Roles
- QA Lead
- QA Engineers
- Automation Engineers

### Responsibilities by Role
See [Team Roles](team-roles.md)

## How We Work

### Core Principles
- **Quality first**: Never compromise on quality
- **Test early**: Shift left in development
- **Automate**: Reduce manual effort
- **Collaborate**: Work closely with development
- **Document**: Make testing reproducible
